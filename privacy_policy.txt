ShiftController Holidays – Privacy Policy
==========================================

Last updated: May 2026

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All settings (holiday dates, background color, enabled/disabled state) are stored
locally in your browser using the chrome.storage.sync API, and are only used to
highlight columns in ShiftController4 pages.

No data is sent to any external server.
No analytics or tracking of any kind is used.

Developer contact: (none)

orphans or fighting hunger in the world, you may donate to:
